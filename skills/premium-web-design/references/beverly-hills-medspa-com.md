## Hero Section
Prominent hero image with powerful tagline "Elevating Existing Beauty" establishing luxury positioning. Professional imagery paired with aspirational messaging. Large visual anchor immediately communicates premium brand positioning without text-heavy content.

## Navigation
Clean, hierarchical mega-menu structure with service categories:
- Face (Rejuvenate your complexion)
- Body (Shape & transform your body)
- Hair
- Skin
- Wellness

Each category features descriptive subtitles explaining benefits. "TOP" badges highlight premier treatments. Concern-based filtering provides alternative discovery path (e.g., "Tired eyes" or "Stubborn fat"). Mobile-responsive menu toggle for smaller screens.

## Scroll Animations
Smooth scroll behavior with content-driven reveals. Before/after galleries trigger visual interest without distraction. Subtle transitions between content sections. No heavy parallax or complex animation sequences.

## Hover & Interactions
