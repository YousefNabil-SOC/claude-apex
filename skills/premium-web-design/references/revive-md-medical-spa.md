# Revive Med Spa - Design Reference

**URL:** https://revive.md/
**Analyzed:** 2026-04-23
**Overall Rating:** 4.5/5

## Tech Stack
- Framework: Next.js (inferred from /_next/image optimization)
- Image Optimization: Next.js image optimization with responsive sizing
- Animation: Subtle, minimal (focus on performance)
- Backend: Modern healthcare web stack
- Booking System: Zenoti or similar
- Compliance: LegitScript verification badge

## Hero Section
Large, high-quality lifestyle imagery as primary visual anchor. Initial hero features "Black Friday Savings" seasonal promotions. Contextual photography (women with glowing skin, outdoor wellness scenes) establishes aspirational positioning. Responsive image sizing (w=3840&q=75 parameters) optimizes for multiple screen sizes.

## Navigation
Main menu hierarchy: Our Treatments, Your Concerns, Packages, Locations, About, Promotions & Events. Navigation guides users toward booking or learning more. Sticky "Book Now" button in top navigation and multiple "Book a Free Consultation" links throughout page reduce friction.

## Scroll Animations
Embedded Instagram feed previews with "View Post" links suggest subtle integration animations. Smooth scroll behavior guides visual narrative from promotions through treatment discovery to booking CTA.

## Hover & Interactions
- "Book Now" button (sticky top position)
- "Book a Free Consultation" links throughout
- Treatment selection dropdown ("What is your primary concern?")
- Location filtering and expansion (Southern/Northern California segmentation)
- Instagram feed integration with "View Post" links
- CTA buttons with consistent styling and placement

## Typography & Color
Minimalist design with clean white space. Sans-serif typography emphasizes professionalism and modernity. Tagline "Love Your Skin" suggests warm, approachable branding. Accent colors guide attention to conversion elements (booking buttons).

## Page Transitions
Standard SPA transitions from page to page. Smooth scrolling behavior creates cohesive experience. Location-based filtering enables seamless clinic selection without page reloads.

## Unique Elements
Prominent display of social proof: "13,000+ 5-star reviews" builds credibility. Instagram feed integration shows real patient results and engagement. Location expansion allowing clinic-specific browsing. LegitScript verification badge demonstrates regulatory compliance transparency. Before/after galleries showcase treatment results.

## Reusable Patterns
1. Hero with large lifestyle imagery (aspirational positioning)
2. Sticky "Book Now" button for persistent conversion CTA
3. Social proof display (13,000+ 5-star reviews)
4. Treatment selector dropdown ("What is your primary concern?")
5. Location filtering and expansion for multi-location clinics
6. Instagram feed integration for social proof and real results
7. Multiple booking pathway options (direct vs. consultation request)
8. "Not Ready to Book?" friction reducer copy
9. Navigation hierarchy: Treatments > Concerns > Packages > Locations
10. Professional imagery optimization with responsive sizing
11. LegitScript verification badge for compliance transparency
12. Expandable service menus for large treatment catalogs

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- Avoid heavy animations that delay booking CTAs
- Excessive promotional content can distract from core service discovery
- Over-reliance on Instagram integration without backup content strategy
- Complex location selection when single-location clinics dominate the space
